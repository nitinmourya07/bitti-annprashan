Annaprashan Vidhi

अन्नप्राशन — पहला अन्न संस्कार

Annaprashan Hindu dharm ka ek pavitra sanskar hai jisme baby ko pehli baar anna (solid food) khilaya jaata hai. Yeh ceremony baby ke 5th-6th month mein hoti hai. Is din baby ko kheer ya meetha chaawal khilaya jaata hai — jo uske swasth aur shubh jeevan ki kamna ka pratik hai. 🌾

Step by Step

Ceremony Rituals

Step 01
Snan — स्नान
शुद्धि स्नान
Baby ko pavitra jal se snan karate hain — shubh vastra pehnate hain. Ek nayi shuruaat ki tayyaari.
Step 02
Puja — पूजा
गणेश पूजन एवं हवन
Pandit ji ke saath puja hoti hai — Ganesh ji ka aahvaan, havan, mantra path. Baby ke swasth jeevan ke liye prarthna.
Step 03
Anna Prashan — अन्नप्राशन
पहला कौर
Sabse khaas moment! Baby ko pehli baar kheer ya meethe chaawal khilaye jaate hain — usually Maa ya Nani ke haathon se.
Step 04
Aashirwad — आशीर्वाद
बड़ों का आशीर्वाद
Dada-Dadi, Nana-Nani, sab bade apna aashirwad dete hain. Baby ke sar pe haath rakhte hain.
Step 05
Bhoj — भोज
परिवार का भोजन
Ceremony ke baad poora parivar saath mein bhojan karta hai — khushi manate hain, mithai batate hain!
🥣

Pehla Kaur — Kheer

Baby ko pehli baar meethi kheer ya payesh khilaya jaata hai.
Yeh shubh bhojan ka pratik hai — meetha jeevan, meethi shuruat. 🍚✨
Chandi ke chamach se, maa ke haathon se — pehla kaur baby ke honthon pe!

ॐ अन्नपतये नमः।
अन्नं ब्रह्मेति व्यजानात्।
"Om Annapataye Namah — Anna (food) hi Brahma hai — food is divine."
Taittiriya Upanishad se — bhojan ki pavitrata ka shloka.
